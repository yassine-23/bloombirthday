Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure availability, especially for weekends and holiday seasons.

Do you provide tables and chairs?

Our basic packages include decoration of your existing furniture. Tables and chairs can be provided as an add-on service at additional cost.

How early do you set up before the event?

We typically arrive 3-4 hours before your event to ensure everything is perfectly set up before your guests arrive.

Do you offer custom themes not shown in your gallery?

Absolutely! We love creating custom themes. Share your ideas with us, and we'll work together to bring your vision to life.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7+ days before the event receive a full refund minus the deposit. Cancellations within 3-6 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 48 hours before the event are non-refundable.
